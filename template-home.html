HOW IT WORKS

We all have memories and moments in our lives that are precious to us. Little moments that perfectly define who we are, and the events that helped to shape the person we have become. What if we could save these moments and share them with friends and family we love even long after we are gone. While we might not be able to capture all those moments, we can capture plenty with the help of My Life’s Legacy. With My Life’s Legacy, you can create an entire digital life story of photos, videos, audio, and text for the next generation to have and to cherish. Your legacy can hold limitless memories and life events to pass on to your children and grandchildren and beyond. We offer this service for a one-time purchase with no recurring costs or fees, and it will stay private until you have passed on.

ABOUT MY LIFE LEGACY

If you are reading this page If you are reading this page you have probably seen the video on the home page explaining what this site is about.

You already know what I look and sound like. You also know a little about my life and why we’ve started this site. This is kind of what My life’s Legacy is all about. To be able to see and hear someone you may or may not have ever met, and to be able to understand what their life was like while they were here. The things that were important to them, their families, their work, their passions.

Our commitment is to preserve the life history of all the members of my life’s legacy for generations to come so that every life is remembered, your way. My Life’s Legacy is set up so that it will still be here long after you and I are gone, but not forgotten. We understand that you are entrusting your legacy, your life history, to us, and we will take great pride in preserving your legacy, and helping people discover or reconnect with a part of their past, their history.
